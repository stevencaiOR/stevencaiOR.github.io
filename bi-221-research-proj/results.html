Conclusion
Based on the data, our results are inconclusive. The slug that supported the greatest nematode diversity weighed 0.9 grams at 10 unique species. Following that, there were two slugs that hosted 5 unique nematode species each at 0.8 grams and 2.2 grams. On the other hand, the largest slugs in terms of mass were 20.2 grams and 19.3 grams respectively, which hosted 0 and 4 unique slug species respectively.
